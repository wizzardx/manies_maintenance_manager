About Us

The idea behind this website is to function like an interactive spreadsheet for each property agent, allowing both Marnie and the agents to track and manage each job in a row of the spreadsheet.

The purpose of this "spreadsheet" is to assist Marnie and property developers with arranging maintenance and repairs to homes. It helps in documenting and tracking the full lifecycle of a property maintenance job, including:

  • Initial callout, inspection, and quote
  • Acceptance of the quote, details of the completed job, invoice, and proof of payment
  • Upload, storage, and retrieval of relevant PDF files
  • Emailing of relevant parties as a maintenance job is submitted or progresses further
